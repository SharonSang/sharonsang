This is our product.

Zalisha is an online market place for agricultural produce which offers agricultural advice to farmers.

The application offers farmers better and accurate predictive farming information depending on their various locations, by providing accurate soil, crop and climate data through a farmer portal that also keeps track of their farming activities.

Zalisha will also connect farmers directly to the local consumer market giving farmers and cooperative groups ability to sell their produce easily

Zalisha also provides a communication channel for farmers through formation of forums and groups.
